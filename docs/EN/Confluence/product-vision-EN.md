# Product Vision — Taxi App

---

## The Problem

Ordering a taxi by phone is slow and unpredictable. The passenger calls a dispatcher, waits on hold, does not know the price in advance, and has no idea when the driver will arrive. The process depends entirely on a human — and humans make mistakes.

---

## The Solution

Taxi App is a mobile platform that connects passengers with nearby drivers in seconds. The passenger opens the app, enters a destination, and confirms the order — the driver arrives in under 5 minutes.

Everything is simple:
- The passenger sees the price **before** confirming the order
- The driver arrives in **under 5 minutes**
- Payment by card or cash — the passenger chooses
- The driver's location is tracked on the map in real time

---

## Who It Is For

| User | What they get |
|------|---------------|
| Passenger | Fast ride ordered in a few taps, transparent price, no phone calls |
| Driver | Automatic order flow, no dispatcher, steady income |
| Administrator | Full control over tariffs, drivers, and reports in one web panel |

---

## Key Success Metric

**Average driver waiting time ≤ 5 minutes.**

This is the main indicator that the service works. If the driver arrives in under 5 minutes — the passenger comes back. If not — they switch to a competitor.

---

## What Makes This Different

The system is built for simplicity. The passenger completes the entire order flow in 4 steps: open app → enter address → select category → confirm. No registration required to see prices. No surprises after the ride.
