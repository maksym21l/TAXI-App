# Business Requirements — Taxi App

---

## Problem Description

Passengers currently order taxis in two ways: by phone call to a dispatcher or through third-party apps (Uklon, Uber). Both options have limitations — a phone call is slow and depends on human factors, while large platforms are not always available or affordable in every city.

Drivers work without a unified platform that automatically distributes orders, tracks rides, and generates reporting. Administrators have no tool to manage tariffs and monitor service operations in real time.

---

## Current Issues

| # | Issue | Impact |
|---|-------|--------|
| 1 | Ordering by phone call — slow and dispatcher-dependent | Passenger waits longer than necessary |
| 2 | No automatic matching with the nearest driver | Inefficient order distribution, long waiting times |
| 3 | Ride price is not transparent — passenger learns the cost only after the ride | Low trust in the service |
| 4 | Cash-only payment in some services | Inconvenient for passengers without cash |
| 5 | No mutual rating system for driver and passenger | No service quality control |
| 6 | Administrator has no tool for ride monitoring and tariff management | Manual work, no analytics |

---

## Business Goal

Build a mobile platform that automatically connects passengers with the nearest available drivers, provides transparent pricing, online payment, and quality control through a rating system — to reduce waiting time and increase the number of daily rides.

---

## Business Objectives

| # | Objective | Measurement |
|---|-----------|-------------|
| 1 | Reduce driver waiting time | Average waiting time ≤ 5 minutes |
| 2 | Provide transparent pricing | Passenger sees ride cost before confirming the order |
| 3 | Enable online payment | 100% of card transactions processed through the system |
| 4 | Ensure service quality control | Driver rating visible to passenger before the ride |
| 5 | Grow active driver base | From 0 to 50+ active drivers per city within 3 months |

---

## Expected Result

After launch, passengers order taxis through the mobile app in seconds, see the price and driver before the ride, and pay by card or cash. Drivers receive orders automatically without phone calls. The administrator manages tariffs and views statistics in one place.
